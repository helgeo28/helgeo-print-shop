About sublimation printing

Vibrant and Permanent Colors
Vibrant and Permanent Colors
Sublimation printing produces incredibly vibrant and long-lasting colors. The ink becomes part of the fabric or substrate, resulting in a fade-resistant and durable print.
Photo-Quality Printing
Photo-Quality Printing
It is an ideal choice for printing high-resolution images, photographs, and intricate designs with fine details.
Washability
Washability
Sublimated prints are highly resistant to fading or cracking when subjected to washing, making them suitable for sportswear and other items that require frequent cleaning.
Durability
Durability
Sublimation prints are known for their durability, as they are less prone to peeling, fading, or cracking compared to some other printing methods.
